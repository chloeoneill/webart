Chloe's Top 15 Questions

MY 15 FAVORITE QUESTIONS

My Questions

  1. What can I do to integrate my photography/writing with my graphic design?
  2. How can I post to social media/website without worrying about people stealing my work?
  3. How can I improve my resume to showcase both my photo work and my design work?
  4. Where to find inspiration/archives outside of where I usually look?
  5. How much of my work should I include in my portfolio/website?
  6. What is the best form for a portfolio? (when applying for jobs)
  7. At what point am I sacrificing my art for the money?
  8. How can I utilize tech/internet space to my advantage? (besides social media and website)
  9. How can I separate my design practice from capitalistic, soul sucking, corporate design?
  10. What are the best ways/practice of documenting my process?
  11. How can I improve my file management/backups?
  12. At what point should I sacrifice my photographic practice for graphic design? (or vice versa)
  13. How can I articulate my "look" as an artist/designer?
  14. How can I be successful this semester/year/academic career?
  15. How do I define success?

Classmate Questions

  1. Where can I go to visit traditional instruments from different countries?
  2. Will I understand coding months after this class?
  3. Is it smart to not be tied to one medium?
  4. How do you stay focused and passionate about your work?
  5. How can I integrate tech in the mediums I already use?
  6. If you found an internship outside your field, should you accept it?
  7. Pertaining to art and history, does photography contain the most voracity?
  8. Art made with a lot of detail versus not?
  9. Do you think that people creating their own personal websites instead of creating social media profles, does that make their information more valuable?
  10. Are you self-centered?
  11. How do we do with moral implications of the art world?
